About the Festival

A Decade of Musical Dialogue Between Two Worlds

Since 2011, the Europe–China Arts Festival — now officially renamed Music Meets Europe — has successfully held ten editions, becoming an important platform for cultural exchange between China and Europe.

In 2026, Music Meets Europe will travel across Europe, offering participants the unique opportunity to perform in world-renowned cultural cities, building friendships across borders through the power of music.
